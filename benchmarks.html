Methodology — Not the Same Thing Being Measured
AA field: per-task isolated runs on SWE-Bench-Pro-Hard-AA, Terminal-Bench v2, and SWE-Atlas-QnA benchmarks. Each task = one bug/issue, agent runs in clean session. MO§ES: sustained operator work on a real product (Application Hub) over a 7-day measurement window (2026-05-08 → 2026-05-14) — 21 sessions, 7,327 turns, 35,242 LOC shipped in 5 build days. Task-equivalents derived from 7,327 ÷ 5 turns/task = 1,465 tasks. $/LOC: AA = cost_per_task ÷ 20 LOC (industry SWE-Bench convention). MO§ES = $23.33/wk subscription ÷ 35,242 actual LOC = $0.000662. The convergence of leadership across both regimes — isolated benchmark runs and sustained product builds — is the structural result.
